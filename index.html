India

The northern frontier of India is dominated by the Himalayas, the world’s highest mountain range. Peaks like Mount Everest and Kanchenjunga soar high into the sky, offering breathtaking vistas. The region’s snow-capped mountains, verdant valleys, and alpine meadows provide some of the most stunning scenery in the world. Places like Leh-Ladakh, Shimla, and Manali are renowned for their dramatic landscapes and adventure opportunities.
